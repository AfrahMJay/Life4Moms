REDUCING MATERNAL MORTALITY IN NIGERIA

The Problem

WHO estimated that 1 woman dies every 4 minutes from pregnancy, pregnancy-related complications and childbirth worldwide. Nigeria contributes 14% to the global burden of maternal mortality with a mortality ratio of about 576 deaths in 100,000 live births. These deaths are preventable but they are not detected early enough because the women are not financially buoyant to attend the antenatal and postnatal clinics.

Our Solution

mInsure is a micro-insurance scheme under Life4Moms that seeks to reduce maternal mortality by providing financial covers for pregnant women of low socioeconomic status. We partner with primary health care centers and get the antenatal, delivery and post-natal bills covered for these women. We also educate them so they would be able to recognize complications long before they become fatal and seek medical care.
